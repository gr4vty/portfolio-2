About

Hello,
I am Josh Eberhart, a professional front-end web developer and graphic designer. I specialize in the development of both static and Wordpress based websites utilizing custom HTML, CSS and JavaScript through the tried and true Bootstrap framework.

I have a decade worth of experience as a US Marine, where I served as an Intelligence Analyst on multiple deployments overseas and in support of major projects in the USA.
